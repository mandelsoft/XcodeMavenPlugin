Maven XCode Plug-in

In the next months we (SAP) will provide the sources of our Maven XCode plug-in, which is already used productively at SAP, on github.

The Maven plug-in allows to build iOS XCode projects with Maven and supports important features like (iOS library) dependency management.

More information will be published on this page soon...
Please subscribe to our mailinglist maven4ios@freelists.org to stay up-to-date.
